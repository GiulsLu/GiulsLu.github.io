## About Me

I am a second-year PhD student in Machine Learning at <a href="https://www.ucl.ac.uk/"> UCL </a>, London. I work under the supervision of <a href="http://www0.cs.ucl.ac.uk/staff/M.Pontil/">M. Pontil</a>  and <a href="https://cciliber.github.io">C. Ciliberto</a>

## Research Interests

My research is focused on the interplay of optimal transport and machine learning.
